Mario Infinite Lives Exploit

Description

The infinite lives exploit is an exploit in a staple of gaming culture Super Mario Bros for the original NES. It allows players to gain infinite lives. The exploit utilizes the games feature rewarding 1up for score in the original game. All the player needs is a nearby stair case as well as a koopa coming down the stairs. This exploit is one of the most well known in gaming culture as it derrives from a game that started it all.

How it’s Done

To perform the infinite lives exploit in the original Super Mario Bros game, you need to take out the first Koopa and kick him off. Then, stomp on the second Koopa when he reaches the block just one step above you. The key is to be in the right spot and hit the second Koopa at the right time. If you succeed in doing this, you‘ll repeatedly jump and kick him into the block. It‘s all about placement. Just keep experimenting and you should succeed. By following these steps, you can gain infinite lives in the game. Have fun playing!
